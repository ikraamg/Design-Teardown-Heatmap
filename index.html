Person Of The Week

Tracy Lee (@LadyLeet) is a JavaScript developer, Google Developers Expert, and entrepreneur who loves to build companies and communities. She co-founded the framework agnostic JavaScript mentoring and consulting firm This Dot Labs and, to report on the latest improvements in the JavaScript ecosystem, she organizes This.JavaScript. Tracy also runs Contributor Days to foster and grow open-source communities, she’s part of the RxJS core team, and advises to Vue Vixens. Thank you for everything you do for the community, dear Tracy!
